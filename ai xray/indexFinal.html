The Challenge

Why Facial X-Rays Often Miss the Mark

Even the slightest shift in head position during a facial X-ray can render the image unusable. This forces technicians to repeat scans — increasing radiation exposure, delaying diagnoses, and raising costs for both patients and providers.

  • Repeat Scans → More radiation exposure
  • Specialized Hardware → Costly and hard to scale
  • Manual Adjustments → Time-consuming and inconsistent

⚠️ Up to 30% of facial X-rays need to be retaken due to misalignment — increasing risk and cost.

THE AI-POWERED SOLUTION

Our system ensures precise, efficient, and safer imaging using advanced AI capabilities.

Single-Camera Alignment

AI detects and corrects facial positioning in real time — removing the need for manual adjustments and reducing setup time.

Voice-Guided Assistance

Clear, step-by-step voice prompts guide patients into the correct posture, improving comfort and scan accuracy.

Automated X-Ray Capture

The scan is triggered only when perfect alignment is achieved, eliminating unnecessary retakes and reducing radiation exposure.

How the AI-Powered X-Ray System Works

Patient Positioning

AI camera aligns the patient’s face in real time for accurate scans.

Voice-Guided Instructions

Provides audio guidance to help patients hold the correct posture.

Intelligent Scan Trigger

Automatically captures scans at the perfect moment.

Instant Output

Generates clear images instantly, reducing repeat scans.
